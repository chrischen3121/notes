* Demand&Supply
** Demand
*** Factors
   - $Q_x$: quantity demanded of $x$
   - $P_x$: price of $x$
   - $I$: income
   - $P$: preference
**** $P_r$: price of related good
    - substitute
    - complement
*** Demand Function
   $$Q_d = f(P_x, P_r, I, P)$$

** Supply
*** Factors
    - $P_x$: price of $x$
    - $P_i$: price of inputs
    - $Tech$: Technology
*** Supply Function
